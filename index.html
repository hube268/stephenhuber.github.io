About Me

Bringing Extreme STEM to the Classroom

I'm a visionary STEM education leader with over 10 years of experience designing and directing programs that give K–12 students access to cutting-edge science and engineering—from experiments aboard the International Space Station to deep-ocean research deployments.

As Director of Innovation at Valley Christian Schools in San Jose, CA, I lead multi-disciplinary engineering teams comprising students, faculty, and industry professionals. I cultivate partnerships with NASA, MBARI, and leading technology companies to create authentic research experiences that go far beyond the textbook.

My career spans aerospace, oceanographic research, competition robotics, IoT engineering, and humanitarian work in active conflict zones—giving me a uniquely broad perspective on what it means to build teams that solve real-world problems under pressure.
